  • How to rip DVDs and Blu-rays on a Mac.
    This will focus on using MakeMKV, Mp4Split, and Mp4Autotag to make professional backups of DVDs and Blu-ray videos. First, we will use MakeMKV to "rip" the content of media with as-little-as-possible lossy compression. Then, using Mp4Split we will compress and encode the content to mp4 for playback on as-many-as-possible different players. Along the way we will use Mp4Split to pull out the different Chapters for Show Episodes (if we have too), and also use Mp4Autotag to add the meta-data to the MP4 so it shows up in iTunes just like a episode or movie you buy though iTunes Store.
