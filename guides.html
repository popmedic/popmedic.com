  • How to back-up DVD movies and tv series on a Mac OSX > 10.7.0.
    This will focus on using Mp4DVD, Mp4Split, and Mp4Autotag to make professional back-ups of DVD movies and tv series. First, we will use Mp4DVD to back-up the content of media with as-little-as-possible lossy compression. Then, compress and encode the content to mp4 for playback on as-many-as-possible different players. Along the way we will use Mp4Split to pull out the different Chapters for Show Episodes (if we have too), and also use Mp4Autotag to add the meta-data to the MP4 so it shows up in iTunes just like a episode or movie you buy though iTunes Store.
